# Selected Approach: "The Terracotta Portal"

I have selected **"The Terracotta Portal"** as it best aligns with the prompt's specific request for a "Portal Terracota" hero section and a "luxurious, terracotta-inspired universe". This approach allows us to combine the requested "modern UI" with "elegance" through the use of architectural forms (arches) and cinematic lighting, creating a truly immersive "app-like" experience rather than just a flat website.

**Design Philosophy:**
We are building a digital gateway to the couple's big day. The user isn't just reading information; they are entering a warm, curated space. The aesthetic balances the raw, earthy nature of terracotta with the refined, sharp elegance of high-end editorial design.

**Key Decisions:**
*   **Visuals:** We will use generated 3D-style abstract renders of terracotta arches and lighting for the Hero to establish the "Portal" theme immediately.
*   **Typography:** *Playfair Display* for that classic wedding elegance, paired with *Inter* for modern readability.
*   **Motion:** Heavy use of Framer Motion for scroll-triggered reveals and parallax effects to simulate depth.
*   **Colors:** We will strictly adhere to the provided palette, using the Dark Mode (Soft Black background) as the default "Luxury/Night" mode for the Hero and Closing, and the Light Mode (Off-white/Nude) for the content-heavy sections (Details, Story) to ensure readability.
